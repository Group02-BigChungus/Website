About

Since the beginning of the Covid-19 breakout, mainstream social media took action to prevent the spread of false or possibly harmful information related to the health emergency. As a result, dark platforms, such as Bitchute, have become more and more popular among users as they allow them to share unsupervised content related to these controversial topics. But what happens once you enter these dark platforms?

The lack of regulation and moderation on these websites makes it very easy for users to switch from ordinary to extreme content in just a few clicks, risking getting lost in a maze of misinformation.
Our project explores four different unbiased entry points related to the Covid-19 topic and, for each one, it shows how that word can lead the user into frenzied chaos of misleading content.
