About

Since the beginning of the Covid-19 breakout, mainstream social media (es. Twitter, Facebook, Youtube) have started to take action to prevent the spread of false or possibly harmful information related to the health emergency. As a result, dark platforms, such as Bitchute, have become more and more popular among users as they allow them to share unsupervised videos related to the pandemic situation. But what happens once you stumble upon this type of content?

The lack of regulation and moderation on these websites makes it very easy for users to switch from neutral to much more extreme content in just a few clicks, risking getting lost in a cloud of misinformation.
The project explores seven different unbiased hashtags related to the Covid-19 topic and, for each one, it shows how that word can lead the user into frenzied chaos of misleading content.

#Masks #Vaccine #Hospital #Lockdown #PCR #CovidPass #WorldHealthOrganization
