#MASKS

Following the neutral hashtag #masks, the platform easily leads the user from institutional videos to more characterised ones that try to appeal to the viewer's emotional side, be it fear or anger. on the website, it is easy to come across videos claiming that masks are useless or even dangerous because they worsen the quality of the air we breathe, as a result, people wearing them are often referred to as #covidiots. It is often said that masks can cause physical or even psychological harm to the wearer, especially to children. Some speakers in the videos also question the legality of making the wearing of masks compulsory, using #scamdemic or even #illuminati to claim that the whole health emergency is false or part of a bigger conspiracy, to the point that people have protested against their compulsory use in public spaces such as schools and shops, verbally attacking the wearers of masks.
